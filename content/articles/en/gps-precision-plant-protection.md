## What Is RTK GPS?

**RTK (Real-Time Kinematic)** is a technology for real-time GPS signal correction that achieves centimeter accuracy.

### RTK System Components

<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th>Component</th>
<th>Function</th>
<th>Characteristics</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>📡 Base station</td>
<td>Reference point</td>
<td>Fixed position, broadcasts corrections</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>📱 Rover (drone)</td>
<td>Receiver</td>
<td>Receives base corrections in real time</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>📶 Data link</td>
<td>Communication</td>
<td>Radio or internet (NTRIP)</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
