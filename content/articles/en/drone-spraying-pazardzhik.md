## Why Pazardzhik Province Is Ideal for Drone Spraying

### Variety of Crops on Compact Areas

The Pazardzhik area is a district of small and medium farms that grow many different crops – from tomatoes and peppers to strawberries and peaches. These farms typically work between 10 and 200 decares with different crops spread across small plots.

With such a structure, conventional equipment – tractors with mounted sprayers – is inefficient and expensive. The DJI Agras T50 is perfect for small and medium plots: it manoeuvres easily, is quickly configured for different crops, and does not require tramline paths.

### Sensitive Crops with High Requirements

Vegetables and fruits are considerably more sensitive than cereal crops – both to diseases and pests, and to the substances themselves and the way they are applied. The drone provides:

- **Fine atomisation** – without coarsely drenching the plants
- **Uniform coverage** – every plant receives an equal dose
- **Precise flow rate control** – without overflow or under-treatment
- **Minimal drift** – the preparation lands exactly where it is needed

### Terrain and Accessibility

The southern parts of the province, towards Velingrad and Batak, are semi-mountainous and mountainous. Orchards and strawberry plantations in these areas are often on sloping terrain, difficult to access with ground machinery. The drone flies and is not affected by slope – it successfully processes any area.

## Crops in Pazardzhik Province Suitable for Drone Spraying

### Tomatoes – the Symbol of Pazardzhik

Pazardzhik Province is a traditional area for growing open-field tomatoes. This crop requires systematic crop protection – from the early stages through to harvest. The main threats are late blight (Phytophthora), early blight, and tomato leafminer.

[Vegetable spraying with a drone](/en/blog/vegetable-spraying-with-drone) ensures precise application of fungicides and insecticides without damaging the delicate stems and fruits. The DJI Agras T50 works with a fine mist that covers plants uniformly – including the underside of leaves, where pests often hide.

### Peppers

Peppers are another leading crop for the region. They are susceptible to many diseases – bacterial spot, fusarium, powdery mildew. Regular fungicide treatments are mandatory for a good harvest. With a drone, these treatments are carried out quickly and without trampling the inter-rows.

### Cucumbers

Open-field cucumbers require protection against downy mildew and powdery mildew. Drone spraying allows timely treatment even after rain, when the soil is wet and ground machinery cannot enter the field.

### Strawberries and Raspberries

Strawberries and raspberries are high-value crops grown on small areas with intensive care. With these crops, manual spraying is slow and uneven, while tractor sprayers are too rough. The DJI Agras T50 is the golden mean – fast, precise and gentle.

### Apples and Peaches

[Orchard spraying with a drone](/en/blog/orchard-spraying-with-drone) is among the most sought-after services in Pazardzhik. Fruit trees require numerous treatments – from winter sprays to summer treatments against fruit moths and scab. The drone provides excellent top-down coverage of tree crowns, which is difficult to achieve with ground sprayers.

### Lavender and Roses

In the northern parts of the province, near Panagyurishte and Strelcha, aromatic crops – lavender and roses – are cultivated. These delicate crops require gentle crop protection, which the drone provides perfectly.

## Advantages of Drone Spraying for Vegetable Growers

### Zero Trampling – Critical for Vegetables

In vegetable production, trampling is one of the biggest problems. Ground sprayers inevitably damage plants in the rows they pass through. The drone completely eliminates this problem – not a single plant is damaged during treatment.

### Speed During Favourable Windows

Vegetables often require spraying within strictly defined windows – early in the morning before the heat or in the evening. With the DJI Agras T50 and productivity of up to 320 decares per hour, even small farms can be treated quickly at the optimal time.

### Precise Dosing for Sensitive Crops

For vegetables, overdosing of a preparation can cause phytotoxicity – burning of leaves and damage to fruits. The DJI Agras T50 provides precise dosing with a variation below 5%, which minimises the risk of damage.

### Ability to Apply Biological Preparations

Many vegetable growers are switching to biological crop protection agents. The drone is ideal for applying biopreparations, which require fine atomisation and uniform coverage for maximum effectiveness.
