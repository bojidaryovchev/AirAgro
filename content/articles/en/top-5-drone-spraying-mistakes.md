## Mistake #3: Improper Mixing and Concentration

### The dangers

<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th>Mistake</th>
<th>Problem</th>
<th>Solution</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>🧪 Over-concentrated</td>
<td>Phytotoxicity, leaf burn</td>
<td>Follow label exactly</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>💧 Too diluted</td>
<td>Low effectiveness, retreatment needed</td>
<td>Use a calculator</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>🚫 Wrong mixing order</td>
<td>Sediment, nozzle clogging</td>
<td>Add product to water (not reverse!)</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>💦 Dirty water (pH >8)</td>
<td>Active ingredient degradation</td>
<td>pH 6-7, filtered water</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

### ✅ Calculation formula

**Example for 10 hectares of wheat:**

```
Product: 1.5 L/ha → 15 L total
Water: 15 L/ha → 150 L total
Concentration: 15L / 150L = 10% solution
```

**Mixing rules:**

1. ✅ Always add product to water
2. ✅ Use clean water with pH 6-7
3. ✅ Stir well before filling
4. ✅ Filter the solution (100 mesh filter)
