## Plovdiv Province – an Agricultural Powerhouse of Bulgaria

### The Thracian Plain – the Breadbasket of the Country

Plovdiv Province encompasses a significant part of the Upper Thracian Plain – a vast flatland with alluvial soils formed by the Maritsa, Tundzha and their tributaries. The region is characterised by:

- **Fertile alluvial soils** – ideal for diverse crops
- **Continental climate with Mediterranean influence** – a long growing season
- **Abundant water resources** – irrigation systems from the Maritsa and reservoirs in the area
- **Rich agricultural tradition** – generations of farmers with accumulated experience

These conditions make the region perfect for both intensive and extensive farming. That is precisely why AirAgro has established a strong presence in the province and offers fast and reliable drone spraying services in Plovdiv and the surrounding areas.

### Main Crops in Plovdiv Province

The diversity of crops in the region is impressive:

- **Cereals** – wheat, barley, maize
- **Oilseeds** – sunflower, rapeseed
- **Vegetables** – tomatoes, peppers, cucumbers, aubergine, onions (especially in the Maritsa and Sadovo areas)
- **Vineyards** – Plovdiv Province is one of the leading wine-producing provinces in the country
- **Orchards** – peaches, apricots, cherries (especially at the foot of the Rhodopes)
- **Rice** – in the Maritsa river basin

Each of these crops requires a specific approach during treatment, and the DJI Agras T50 provides the flexibility needed for precise work with each of them.

## Advantages of Drone Spraying for Farmers in Plovdiv

### Fast Response in Emergency Situations

The Plovdiv region is subject to sudden pest attacks, especially during the warm summer months. Aphids, thrips and various fungal diseases can cause serious damage within days. With AirAgro you get:

- **Response within 24–48 hours** of the request
- **Fast execution** – the drone is in the field working while traditional sprayers are still being organised
- **No dependence on soil condition** – the drone works even when the field is wet and ground machinery cannot enter

### Precision at Every Operation

The DJI Agras T50 provides RTK navigation with ±2 cm accuracy, 8 nozzles for uniform coverage and automatic dose adjustment. The result:

- Saving 20–30% on preparations compared to conventional spraying
- Uniform coverage of every plant
- No overlap and no gaps

### No Trampling of Crops

Ground sprayers cause trampling of 3–5% of the area with every pass. Over several treatments per year, the yield loss from trampling is significant. The drone flies above the crops and does not touch a single plant.

### Environmental Compliance

Drone spraying complies with the requirements of the EU's Common Agricultural Policy (CAP) and green requirements for sustainable farming. Less preparation, more precise application, less contamination of soil and water.
