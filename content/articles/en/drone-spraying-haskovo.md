## Why Drone Spraying Is Ideal for the Haskovo Region

### Diversity of Crops on Small Plots

Haskovo region is distinguished by great diversity of cultivated crops. Unlike Dobrudzha, where vast grain fields predominate, here the terrain is more fragmented – many farmers work 50–300 decares with different crops. It is precisely under such conditions that drone spraying in Haskovo shows its greatest advantages.

Drones easily switch between different fields and crops. A single DJI Agras T50 can treat a sunflower field in the morning and a vineyard or vegetable garden in the afternoon. This flexibility is impossible with heavy ground machinery, which requires time for preparation and transportation.

### Hilly Terrain and Difficult-to-Access Areas

The southern parts of the region, at the foothills of the Rhodopes, are hilly and difficult to access with conventional machinery. The vineyard tracts near Stambolovo, Lyubimets and Ivaylovgrad are often situated on sloped terrain where tractors work ineffectively or cannot enter at all.

Agricultural drones fly at 2–5 metres above crops and are independent of terrain slope. This makes them the ideal solution for [vineyard spraying with a drone](/en/blog/vineyard-spraying-with-drone) throughout the Haskovo region.

### Climatic Specifics of the Region

The Thracian Plain is characterised by hot and dry summers, which requires fast and timely treatments. When temperatures exceed 30°C, spraying must be performed early in the morning or in the evening. The DJI Agras T50 allows treatment of up to 200 decares per hour, meaning that even in the short optimal spraying windows significant areas can be treated.

## Crops in Haskovo Region Suitable for Drone Spraying

### Tobacco – the Historic Crop of the Region

Haskovo region is a traditional tobacco-growing area. Tobacco is planted on small plots and requires precise crop protection. With drone spraying in Haskovo, tobacco fields are treated with minimal trampling (practically zero), which is decisive for the delicate tobacco plants.

The DJI Agras T50 disperses preparations evenly thanks to its 8 nozzles and intelligent flow control system. The fine mist penetrates deep into the leaf mass, providing effective protection against tobacco thrips, aphids and mildew.

### Cotton – Specific Requirements

Cotton is another characteristic crop of the Haskovo area, requiring multiple treatments during the season – from herbicide treatments to insecticide sprayings against the cotton bollworm. With conventional tractor spraying, trampling losses can reach 5–8% of the harvest.

Drones eliminate this problem completely. They move along pre-set routes with RTK accuracy to 2 cm, never touching the ground.

### Sunflower and Grain Crops

In the northern and flat parts of the region, near Dimitrovgrad and Harmanli, sunflower, wheat and corn predominate. For these larger tracts the drones provide speed and efficiency, especially with urgent treatments against diseases or pests.

### Vegetables and Greenhouse Crops

The region is also known for growing tomatoes, peppers and cucumbers in the open field. [Vegetable spraying with a drone](/en/blog/vegetable-spraying-with-drone) is especially suitable for these sensitive crops, as the drone does not damage plants and provides uniform coverage.

### Vineyards – Stambolovo and Harmanli Region

Stambolovo is one of the most renowned wine regions in Bulgaria. The vineyard tracts here require multiple fungicide and insecticide treatments. [Vineyard spraying with a drone](/en/blog/vineyard-spraying-with-drone) is significantly more effective than manual spraying with backpack sprayers, which is still practised on sloped terrain.
