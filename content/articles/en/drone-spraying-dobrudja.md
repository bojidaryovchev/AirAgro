## Drone Spraying in Dobrudzha – Services for the Breadbasket of Bulgaria

Dobrudzha is the breadbasket of Bulgaria – the region with the highest yields of grain and oil crops in the country. With its vast black-earth fields, intensive agriculture and high requirements for timely crop protection, **drone spraying in Dobrudzha** is the logical next step for modern farms in the region.

AirAgro offers [professional drone spraying services](/en/blog/drone-spraying-services) across the entire Dobrudzha territory – from Dobrich and General Toshevo to Silistra and Tutrakan.

## Dobrudzha – Agricultural Region with Enormous Potential

### The Black Earth – the Region's Wealth

The Dobrudzha black earth is among the most fertile soils in Europe. This soil type provides exceptional conditions for growing grain crops, but simultaneously creates a favourable environment for weeds, diseases and pests. This is precisely why timely and effective crop protection is of critical importance for every Dobrudzha farm.

### Large-Scale Agriculture

Unlike the southern regions of Bulgaria, Dobrudzha is characterised by large, consolidated plots – with plots of 1,000, 3,000 and even 5,000 decares in a single holding not uncommon. This scale requires technology that can work fast without compromising quality. The DJI Agras T50 processes up to 200 decares per hour, making it a powerful tool for the largest farms in the region.

### Intensive Agricultural Calendar

Dobrudzha grain producers work with a busy calendar – from autumn sowing of wheat and rapeseed to spring sowing of corn and sunflower. Each crop requires multiple treatments during the season, and the windows for optimal spraying are often short. Drones enable maximum use of these windows.

## Crops in Dobrudzha and Drone Spraying

### Wheat – the Main Crop

Dobrudzha produces over 40% of Bulgaria's wheat. Winter wheat requires at least 3–4 treatments annually – herbicide spraying, fungicide treatments and foliar feeding. [Wheat spraying with a drone](/en/blog/wheat-spraying-with-drone) allows each of these treatments to be performed at the optimal moment.

The DJI Agras T50 ensures precise distribution thanks to:

- **8 centrifugal nozzles** with controlled droplet size
- **RTK positioning** with accuracy up to 2 cm
- **Automatic flow control** when speed changes
- **Terrain radar sensor** – maintains constant height above crops

With large wheat tracts in Dobrudzha these characteristics guarantee uniform coverage without gaps and overlap.

### Corn – the Second Gold of Dobrudzha

Corn is the second most important crop in the region. It requires herbicide treatments in early phases and insecticide treatments against the European corn borer. [Corn spraying with a drone](/en/blog/corn-spraying-with-drone) is especially effective, because when corn grows tall, ground machinery has difficulty entering the crops without damaging them.

With corn above 1.5 metres in height, the drone is the only way for effective treatment without trampling. The DJI Agras T50 flies at 2–3 metres above the crop and provides excellent preparation penetration down to the lower leaves.

### Sunflower – Key Oil Crop

Sunflower is the third main crop for Dobrudzha. Herbicide and fungicide treatments in the early development phases are critical for good yields. Drone spraying ensures timely treatment without compacting the valuable black-earth soil.

### Rapeseed – the Crop with High Requirements

Rapeseed requires intensive crop protection – from autumn insecticide treatments against the rapeseed flea beetle to spring fungicide sprayings. The speed of the drone is decisive, as the treatment windows for rapeseed often coincide with unfavourable meteorological conditions.

### Barley

Barley, though on smaller areas, is also an important crop for Dobrudzha. Drone spraying provides the same precision and effectiveness as for other grain crops.

## How Drone Spraying Complements Large Farms

### A Complement, Not a Replacement

For large Dobrudzha farms with thousands of decares, drone spraying is not a replacement for ground machinery, but a powerful complement. Here is when the drone is indispensable:

- **Urgent treatments** – when disease or pest appears suddenly and every hour counts
- **Wet terrain** – after rain heavy sprayers cannot enter the field, but the drone works without a problem
- **Supplementary treatments** – for plots missed by ground machinery
- **Foliar fertilization** – precise application of microelements
- **Difficult-to-access corners** – field edges, strips alongside canals and roads

### Speed at Critical Moments

Imagine the scenario: mass grain bug attack at the end of May. A farm with 3,000 decares of wheat needs to be treated within 2–3 days. With the DJI Agras T50 this volume can be processed in 10–12 hours of effective work. The AirAgro mobile team arrives with ready equipment and starts immediately.

### Integration with Precision Agriculture

Many Dobrudzha farms already use GPS navigation and yield maps. The DJI Agras T50 can work with variable-rate spraying maps – applying different doses in different zones of the field. This optimises preparation expenditure and improves treatment effectiveness.

## Advantages of Drone Spraying in Dobrudzha

### No Soil Compaction

The black earth is sensitive to compaction. Heavy sprayers with full tanks exert enormous pressure on the soil. The drone exerts zero pressure – it does not touch the ground and preserves soil structure.

### Speed and Flexibility

Processing up to 200 decares per hour, the DJI Agras T50 can cover vast areas in a short time. With a combination of several drones this speed increases further.

### Resource Savings

Working with ultra low volume (1–2 L/dka) means less water and preparation consumption. For a farm with 5,000 decares this can mean saving thousands of lev per year.

### Full Documentation

Every spraying is recorded – GPS coordinates, area, flow rate, speed, height. This information is valuable for farm management and for reporting to control authorities.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can the drone replace the ground sprayer for a large Dobrudzha farm?

The drone is not a replacement but a powerful complement to ground machinery. For farms with over 3,000 decares the main work is done by ground sprayers, and the DJI Agras T50 is used for emergencies, wet terrain, difficult-to-access areas and supplementary treatments. For farms up to 1,500 decares the drone can be the primary crop protection tool.

### How does the drone cope with strong wind in Dobrudzha?

The DJI Agras T50 works effectively in wind up to 6 m/s (about 20 km/h). In stronger wind the operator stops work to guarantee quality coverage without preparation drift. In Dobrudzha wind is a frequent factor, so we plan operations for early morning or evening hours when conditions are optimal.

### How many decares can the drone treat per day with large grain tracts?

With large consolidated blocks (500+ decares) the DJI Agras T50 treats up to 200 dka/hour. For an effective working day of 8 hours this means **1,500–2,000 dka**. Using two drones simultaneously, productivity increases to 3,000+ dka/day.
