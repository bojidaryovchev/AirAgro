## Special Cases

### Working in Cherry Orchards (6-8 m height)

**Challenge:**

- Very tall trees
- Dense canopy
- Need for coverage from inside

**Solution with DJI T50:**

- Bidirectional nozzles (upward + sideways)
- Increased flow: 12-15 L/min
- Additional pass if needed

### New Plantations (1-2 years)

**Specifics:**

- Small plants
- Large spacing between rows
- Low leaf mass

**Recommendations:**

- Reduce rate by 40-50%
- Height: 1.5-2 m
- Focus on spot treatment

## Regulatory Requirements

### Certification for Perennial Crops

In Bulgaria required:

- ✅ General drone operator certificate
- ✅ Additional training for vertical spraying
- ✅ Civil liability insurance
- ✅ Compliance with Regulation 13 for PPP

### Buffer Zones

**When spraying vineyards near:**

- Residences: minimum 50 m
- Water bodies: minimum 30 m
- Organic crops: minimum 100 m
- Apiaries: minimum 500 m (consult beekeepers!)

## Tips from Practice

### ✅ Do

1. **Test with water first**
   - Check coverage
   - Adjust height
   - Find optimal speed

2. **Work early morning**
   - Leaves are wet from dew
   - Better adhesion
   - Minimal evaporation

3. **Document**
   - Before/after photos
   - GPS records
   - Weather conditions

### ❌ Avoid

1. **Spraying in strong wind**
   - Drift to neighboring crops
   - Fruit contamination
   - Legal issues

2. **Passing too quickly**
   - Uneven coverage
   - Missed zones
   - Need for re-treatment

3. **Ignoring pre-harvest intervals**
   - Penalties during inspections
   - Ban on product sales
   - Reputational damage
