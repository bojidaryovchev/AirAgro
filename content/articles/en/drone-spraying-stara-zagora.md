## Stara Zagora Province – a Key Agricultural Region

### Geographic and Climatic Advantages

Stara Zagora Province is blessed with excellent conditions for agriculture:

- **Upper Thracian Plain** – an extensive flatland with deep, fertile soils along the Maritsa and its tributaries
- **Sub-Balkan fields** – Kazanlak and Karlovo basins with a unique microclimate
- **Temperate continental climate** – sufficient warmth and moisture for a full growing season
- **Diverse relief** – from plains to hills, allowing cultivation of different crops

These conditions make the region one of the most productive in the country, and the need for effective crop protection is constant.

### Main Crops in Stara Zagora Province

Agriculture in the province is focused on:

- **Wheat and barley** – the main cereal crops, occupying the largest area
- **Sunflower** – the leading oilseed crop in the lowland areas
- **Rapeseed** – an increasingly important winter crop with growing acreage
- **Vineyards** – Stara Zagora Province is a traditional wine-growing district
- **Oil-bearing rose and lavender** – the Rose Valley around Kazanlak is a unique region
- **Maize** – in the irrigated areas along the Maritsa
- **Vegetables** – in smaller but intensively worked areas

Each of these crops can be treated effectively with the DJI Agras T50, whether that means [wheat spraying](/en/blog/wheat-spraying-with-drone) or [sunflower treatment](/en/blog/sunflower-spraying-with-drone).
