## What Is Integrated Pest Management (IPM)?

**Integrated Pest Management (IPM)** is a holistic approach to plant protection that combines:

<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th>Component</th>
<th>Description</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>🔬 Monitoring</td>
<td>Proactive observation and forecasting</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>🌱 Biological methods</td>
<td>Natural enemies of pests</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>🧪 Chemical products</td>
<td>Application only when necessary</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>🚜 Agrotechnics</td>
<td>Optimization of practices</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>🎯 Precision application</td>
<td>Accurate dosing and localization</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

**Main goal:** Minimum chemical load with maximum effectiveness.
